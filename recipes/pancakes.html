Pancakes

You can't go wrong with a simple pancake! A simple meal perfect for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and Butter
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Take the flour, sugar, salt and baking powder and sift those ingredients together
  2. Then, add the wet ingredients and stir to combine
  3. Afterwards, scoop the ingredients onto a pan on a stove and cook for 2-3 minutes. Flip when necessary
  4. Repeat process until pancakes are brown on both sides

Now you're ready to have delicious pancakes! Apply syrup, if wanted, and enjoy!
