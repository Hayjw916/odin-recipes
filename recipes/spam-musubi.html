Spam Musubi!

Back in World War II, spam became a very popular food especially in Hawaii. With how cheap both spam and rice was in Hawaii, it was a no brainer to combine the two into a delicious little snack that you can create at home

Ingredients

  • 12 Ounces Spam
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Nori roasted seaweed (used for sushi)
  • 6 cups cooked sushi rice (WITHOUT vinegar mixture)

Steps

  1. Slice the SPAM into 8-10 slices and mix the oyster sauce, soy sauce, and sugar until sugar is dissolved and add to the bag with the SPAM. Let it marinate for around 15 minutes
  2. Drain off marinade and fry the SPAM on each side over medium heat until slightly crispy.
  3. Place strip of nori on a clean surface. Place musubi mold across middle of nori, then add sushi rice
  4. Finally, Remove mold from the rice and add the cooked SPAM up top. Wrap both sides to the top of the SPAM
